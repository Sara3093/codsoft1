Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of Single Rooms are generally between 37 m2 to 45 m2.

Family Room

A parlour or living room connected eith to or more bedrooms.(A room with one or more bedrooms and a seperate living space). The room size or area of Suite rooms are generally between 70 m2 to 100 m2.

Duplex Room

The most expensive rooms provided by the hotel. Usually only one Suplex suite is available in one single hotel property. Similar to the normal suites, a duplex suite always has one or more bedrooms and a living space with a strong emphasis on grand in-room decoration, high-quality amenities and and supplies, and tailor-made services.
